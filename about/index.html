About

Mert Biricik.

Cybersecurity researcher and incoming Erasmus Mundus Scholar (MSc in Applied Cybersecurity) with hands-on experience in network administration, system deployment, and threat discovery. I run critical IT infrastructure and software rollouts under high-pressure conditions and focus on Zero Trust, Cloud Security, and SASE.

I work at the seam between systems engineering and applied AI: building research tools that survive production loads, hardening the infrastructure that hosts them, and turning sensor data — power grids, EEG signals, sports telemetry — into signals defenders can act on.
