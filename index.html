№ 01

09/2024 — Present

EnerjiVeri — 100 Years of Turkish Energy Data

TUBITAK 1001 — Sole web developer for the national strategic project (No. 323K520)

Subject. A century of national energy production, consumption, and policy data sat fragmented across CSVs and PDFs, unusable for evidence-based policy analysis.

Method. Built a dynamic Turkish-language data portal consolidating 100 years of quantitative and qualitative energy data with time-series charts, sectoral distribution models, and interactive dashboards.

Verdict. Live national portal used by researchers and policy stakeholders under the project "Energy Strategies in the Second Century of the Republic".

№ 02

2024 — 2025

Tune2Travel — Music Videos & Destination Marketing

TUBITAK 1001 — Data infrastructure & NLP pipeline

Subject. Quantifying how music videos shape tourism perception requires large-scale, multi-language social media data plus cultural NLP processing.

Method. Engineered the data infrastructure: YouTube API ingestion, ETL pipelines, NLP and ML models surfacing cultural and sociological signals about destination marketing on social platforms.

Verdict. National research project deliverable enabling marketing & sociology researchers to query patterns across thousands of cross-language music videos.

№ 03

2025 — 2026

Unsupervised Memory Forensics Triage

Volatility3 + Isolation Forest for fileless malware

Subject. Zero-day and fileless malware evade signature-based detection and overwhelm analysts with raw memory artefacts.

Method. Designed an automated memory forensics triage system on top of Volatility3 with unsupervised ML (Isolation Forest) over behavioural and graph-theoretic features to surface anomalous processes.

Verdict. Manuscript under review (Texas, USA). Co-authored with H. Coskun and R. Creutzburg.

Apparatus

  • Volatility3
  • Memory Forensics
  • Isolation Forest
  • Graph Analysis
  • Python
